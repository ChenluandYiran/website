Auto Signer

• Auto Signer is a hand-coloured, themed “Dream Night” robot pen plotter signature machine which is modelled and simulated via Labview. The glorious shiny purple base decorated with red-and-white butterflies casts a colourful glimmer in the peaceful night, imparting a sense of tranquillity to provide a bit of solace from our hectic, everyday lives.

• The system is composed of a base and a 3 DOF robotic arm, controlled and guided by a real-time embedded evaluation board, MyRIO.

• It is a functional machine designed to meet the daily or specialized writing needs on any flat surface.

• Users can produce writing with the appearance they like, or even design their own handwriting characters just like an artist. The auto Signer will unmistakeably address to an envelope or sign their names with a real replaceable pen.
