Mechanical Husky

• The Mechanical Husky with cute silly face is modelled by 3dMax platform and constructed via 3D printing with the manual painting. It works as a watchdog to help users tracking desired targets, such as tracking golf ball when the user plays golf outdoor.

• Camera system: It is designed with optical target located system based on OpenMV.

• Intelligent Motion Tracking: Upon detection of movement, the husky automatically moves to track the target based on PI control, captures the motion while delivering notification in real time.

• Alert system: Once the target is out of the tracking scope, the husky starts barking and pushes current status to the SystemLink cloud for user to review.
