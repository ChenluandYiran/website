Lego Breadboard Backpack

• The purpose of LEGO Backpacks – Breadboard program is to design and fabricate PCB boards to establish connection between the LEGO SPIKE Prime and breadboard with an embedded controller to provide educators with resources surrounding making in education, and promote teacher and student creativity, curiosity, self-motivation, and self-confidence.

• Enclosures: Designed and manufactured LEGO compatible casings to integrate all related components (Pyboard adapter PCB, universal connecterPCB, LEGO connector, breadboard).

• Communication protocol: the LEGO SPIKE Prime communicate with the embedded controller through LPF2. and the whole Backpacks - Breadboard system wirelessly communicate with master controller through TCP socket to achieve remote control.
